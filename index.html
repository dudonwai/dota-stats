About Dudon

Dudon Wai studied engineering at the University of Waterloo and proceeded to move and work in the oil & gas industry. Passionate about solving complex problems, Dudon executed on $100M+ in projects over two years. After an unlikely reunion with a friend, Dudon jumped into web development and started GreyADV in late 2015. In his free time, Dudon seeks new food experiences and enjoys dance music with his girlfriend Deanne. He also supports local Calgary startups and loves meeting like-minded people. Grateful for an amazing community, Dudon makes sure to share what he has learned and pay it forward to other aspiring entrepreneurs.
